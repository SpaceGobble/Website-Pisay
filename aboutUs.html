About PSHS-CRC

Pshs is an attached agency of the Philippine Department of Science and Technology. PSHS is considered as the top science high school in the Philippines and is viewed to be among the best in the ASEAN region by 2016.Philippine Science High School was established in Diliman, Quezon during 1964.
